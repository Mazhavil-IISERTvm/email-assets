Dear Comrades,

A Marvelous Morn' to you all!

Are you looking to return to the charm of yesteryears? Does your ordinary existence lack sparkle (insert emoji)?

Well fear not, for Mazhavil is on the job! Introducing the 4th annual prom night - Ishqya'26: Ulfat-e-Disco! The theme this year? You guessed it - Retro.

With a return to the fashion, the music and the overall good vibes, the night will be more than groovy - it'll be rad. On the roster for a great evening, we have all our crowd favourites as well as several new surprises so -- keep your eyes peeled. Embark on adventurous paths with your friends and dance to the spinning records as the disco lights shine away any worry of the semester. This time, you will be the dancing queen.

Join us on 14th February at the fabled Indoor Stadium to celebrate peace, love and history! Take a pause from your daily schedule and peruse our planned activities (maybe even participate in some) -

Greatest Wingman: We all have that one friend with whom “I (We) Can’t Help Falling in Love” with. What a beauty it would be if those around us could also know them (and maybe hit on them). And that is Presley (I mean, precisely) the goal of this event. So, can you “advertise” your friend well enough to be their ‘Greatest Wingman’?

Open Mic: For all the ‘Bohemians’ who wish to bring out their inner ‘Rhapsody’s, here is your time to shine. Bound us all by a musical spell, carry us in the wind around the flow of your moves, or speak while embracing silence!

Ramp Walk: “When the Empire Strikes Back”, everyone aspires to go Han Solo. Whether you dress up all cute like Yoda, or all geared up like Darth Vader, this is your stage! Go, rock the runway, and May the Force be with you!

Register here: https://forms.gle/8h1MVcoPDiZC41q97

Paper Dance:So, you’ve got the moves, haven’t you…. But how well can you groove to Macarena in your grunges on a piece of paper that keeps growing smaller? Ahh, careful, just don’t let those military boots of yours touch the ground!

Register here: https://forms.gle/qmMifLBM3Mo8oAPF7

Fast and Queerious: Hey Monica, whether you want a Chandler in your life, a Rachel, or a Ross, this event is your time to socialize. You will be randomly grouped with a bunch of people and given time to have a tête-à-tête as you keep switching partners. (Don’t worry. We’ll be around to be the third wheel and keep the conversation going.) Time to make new ‘Friends’!!

Registrations will be done on spot!

Registrations will be open till 10th February. There are limited slots that will be filled on a first-come-first-serve basis, so quick, catch your spot and show us how you jive!

This blast from the past will remind us that we were never alone, not then and not ever. We have always existed and this Ishqya, we celebrate our victories!

Catch you on the flipside,
Love,
Mazhavil.
