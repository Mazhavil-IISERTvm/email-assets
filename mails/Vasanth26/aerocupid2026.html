Attention all!

Calling all lovers of fine-written letters and sweet treats — Aerocupid orders are finally open!

Do you wish to send out your words to your friends or amors? Drop your letter at our Aerocupid boxes in either CDH1 or CDH2 in the following manner:

  1. Write a letter (yay!) with the recipient's name and hostel/lab
  2. Find the Aerocupid box in either CDH1 or CDH2
  3. A sticker sheet with tracking numbers will be available near the box. Stick a tracking number on your letter (and remember your tracking number as well!)
  4. Finally, drop your letter in the box and walk away

Congrats! You have successfully completed the steps to deliver your letter.

What happens next? Our trusty delivery team will give it to your recipient by the next day and spread smiles all over campus. And don’t worry — your letters will remain anonymous, all privacy filters on.

If your loved one has a sweet tooth, what better way to express your devotion than to order a cupcake that will be delivered to their doorstep?
